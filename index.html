Thinking Behind the Game

We are huge fans of Dark Souls and its art style. Initially, we planned a shooter, but later shifted to a multiplayer Dark Souls-inspired PvP game.

Concept
The game will be PvP with different modes like 1v1, 2v2, and more coming soon. It will be a platformer in the Dark Souls theme.

Launch Date
The game is scheduled for release on March 31, 2025. Future updates will come, but no DLCs. However, a Dark Souls Campaign game is planned!

Gameplay Features

- Intense melee combat with swords, axes, and magic.
- Unique character classes with customizable abilities.
- Dark, immersive medieval environments.
- Ranked matchmaking and casual PvP modes.
- Cross-platform play support.
